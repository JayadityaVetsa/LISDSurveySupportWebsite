About LISDSurvey

LISDSurvey is a district-level iOS survey app designed to replace costly third-party tools and Google Forms. It helps administrators collect and visualize student feedback efficiently while encouraging student participation through a smooth interface and real-time results.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 20, 2025

LISDSurvey is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and password.
  • No other personal information is collected.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your email and password are collected only for authentication purposes (to allow you to sign in and use the app). We do not use this information for marketing, advertising, or any other purpose.

3. Data Storage and Security

All authentication data is securely stored and managed by Google Firebase. We do not store your password directly; Firebase handles encryption and security. We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, share, or disclose your personal information to third parties. Your data is only accessible through Firebase for authentication.

5. Your Rights

You may request to delete your account and associated data at any time. To request deletion or support, contact us via the email below.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are made, we will update the "Effective Date" above and make the revised policy available in the app.
